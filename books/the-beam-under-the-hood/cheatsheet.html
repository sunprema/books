Tuning knobs (measure first!)

spawn_opt(fun, [
  {:min_heap_size, 4_000},
  {:fullsweep_after, 10},
  {:message_queue_data, :off_heap}
])

Boot flags: +S schedulers · +SDcpu/+SDio dirty pools · +sbwt busy-wait.

The 30-second summary

Millions of cheap isolated processes, each with a private heap and mailbox. Fair reduction-counted preemption at 4000, one scheduler per core. Copying message passing (big binaries shared off-heap). Per-process generational GC — no stop-the-world. Long native calls go to dirty schedulers. Machines cluster in a full mesh. Stack it all up and one box holds a huge crowd of live connections.
