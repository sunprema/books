Quick Reference

The Cheatsheet

Every formula, constant, and fact used in this book, on one spread. Learn the reasoning in the chapters; use this to keep it sharp.

Physics · mechanics

  • Atwood: a = (m₂−m₁)g / (m₁+m₂)
  • No-time kinematics: v² = u² + 2gh
  • Weight vs height: W = W₀ · R²/(R+h)²
  • Young's modulus: Y = FL / (A·ΔL); use ΔL, not L₁
  • Always check an extreme case (equal masses → a=0).

Physics · fields & waves

  • Charged sphere, outside: E = kQ/r²; inside a conductor, E = 0
  • k = 1/4πε₀ = 9×10⁹ N·m²/C²
  • Fringe width: β = λD/d
  • Resistor bands: 2 digits · ×10ⁿ · tolerance
  • Colours 0–9: Bl Br R O Y Gr Bl V Gy W (“BB ROY of Great Britain…”)

Physics · modern & nuclear

  • Balance mass number (top) & atomic number (bottom) across the arrow
  • Neutron = ¹₀n; it changes mass, not charge
  • de Broglie shortcut: λ = 1.227/√V nm (electron, V in volts)

Chemistry · physical

  • Gas density: ρ = PM/RT — keep M in kg/mol!
  • Partial pressure: Pi = Ptotal·(ni/ntotal)
  • N₂ molar mass = 28 (diatomic), not 14
  • R = 8.314 J mol⁻¹K⁻¹; 0 °C = 273 K

Chemistry · structure

  • bcc (touch along body diagonal): r = (√3/4)a
  • fcc (touch along face diagonal): r = (√2/4)a
  • simple cubic: r = a/2
  • Spin-only moment: μ = √[n(n+2)] BM
  • Cr (Z=24) = [Ar]3d⁵4s¹ → Cr²⁺ = 3d⁴ (n=4)

Chemistry · organic

  • Toluene + Cl₂/hν → side chain (CH₃→CH₂Cl→CHCl₂→CCl₃)
  • Toluene + Cl₂/Lewis acid → ring chlorination
  • CHCl₂ hydrolyses → –CHO (aldehyde); CH₂Cl → –CH₂OH; CCl₃ → –COOH
  • Dehydrohalogenation = β-elimination, follows Zaitsev

Biology · the code

  • A=T (2 H-bonds); G≡C (3 H-bonds)
  • bp spacing 0.34 nm → length = (bp)×0.34 nm
  • Replication → DNA helicase opens; Transcription → RNA polymerase opens
  • EcoRI site: 5′-GAATTC-3′ (palindrome, sticky ends)
  • Restriction sites are palindromic; ligase seals ends

Biology · body & life

  • ECG: P = atrial depol · QRS = ventricular depol · T = ventricular repol
  • Plasmodium: sporozoite infects human; gametocyte infects mosquito
  • Sickle-cell = autosomal recessive, chromosome 11
  • Haemophilia = X-linked recessive (not Y)
  • Robert May: ≈7 million species; ~1.5 million described
  • Penguin & dolphin flippers = analogous → convergent evolution

Strategy

  • +4 correct, −1 wrong, 0 blank
  • Random 4-way guess: E = +¼; eliminate one → +⅔
  • Biology = 50% of marks — protect it
  • Read “incorrect / not / wrong” twice
  • Units first, arithmetic second
