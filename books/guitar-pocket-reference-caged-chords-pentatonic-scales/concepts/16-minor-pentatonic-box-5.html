Closes the loop. Just below Box 1's octave — Box 5 up here is Box 1's shape lower down.

Shape

  • Frets ~14–17: 15–17 / 15–17 / 14–17 / 14–17 / 15–17 / 15–17.
  • Roots (R): 6th str 17, 3rd str 14, 1st str 17.
  • Wraps back into Box 1 an octave higher.

When & why

  • Completes the five-shape cycle around the neck.
  • The whole-neck picture: 1→2→3→4→5→1 (octave up).
  • Screaming high-register lead territory.

Moves & transitions

  • Links Box 4 below and Box 1 (octave up) above.
  • Because it equals Box 1, licks you know transfer straight in.
  • Use it to see the fretboard as one connected map.
Practice tipPlay all five boxes end to end with the metronome, one box per bar, up and down. That's the whole neck in one drill.

“Five shapes, then it repeats forever. Once you see the loop, the fretboard stops being a mystery.”
