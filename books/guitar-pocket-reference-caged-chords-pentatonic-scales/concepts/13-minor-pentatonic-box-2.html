Just above Box 1, sharing its top notes. Links the CAGED A-shape region.

Shape

  • Frets ~7–10: 8–10 / 7–10 / 7–10 / 7–9 / 8–10 / 8–10.
  • Roots (R): 4th str 7 and 2nd str 10.
  • Same five notes as Box 1 — a new fingering, not a new scale.

When & why

  • Moves your soloing up out of Box 1.
  • Sits under the A-form barre position — handy for chord-tone targeting.
  • Bridges Box 1 to Box 3.

Moves & transitions

  • Overlaps Box 1 on the shared frets — slide between them.
  • The root is no longer on the low E — anchor to the 4th-string R.
  • Great for legato runs across strings D–G.
Practice tipPractise the shift: play Box 1, then slide one finger into Box 2 in time with the click. Own the seam, not just the box.

“Boxes aren't cages. The moment Box 2 feels separate, glue it to Box 1 with a slide.”
