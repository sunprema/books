The middle-neck shape. Roots on the 5th and 2nd strings; a small stretch on the B string.

Shape

  • Frets ~9–13: 10–12 / 10–12 / 10–12 / 9–12 / 10–13 / 10–12.
  • Roots (R): 5th str 12 and 2nd str 10.
  • The B-string reaches 10→13 — use the pinky.

When & why

  • Central position for phrasing across the whole neck.
  • Aligns with the CAGED G-shape region.
  • A favourite launch pad for climbing higher.

Moves & transitions

  • Links Box 2 below and Box 4 above.
  • The 3rd-string 9th-fret note is a strong bluesy target.
  • Roll from the B-string stretch straight into Box 4.
Practice tipSet a slow tempo and keep the pinky hovering over the 12–13 stretch so it never scrambles for the note.

“Box 3 is where players finally leave the 5th-fret pocket. Make the pinky earn its keep here.”
