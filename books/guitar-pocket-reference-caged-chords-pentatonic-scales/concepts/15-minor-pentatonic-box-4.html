Up at the 12th fret. Overlaps the CAGED E-shape and leads into Box 5.

Shape

  • Frets ~12–15: 12–15 / 12–15 / 12–14 / 12–14 / 13–15 / 12–15.
  • Roots (R): 5th str 12 and 4th str 14.
  • At the 12th fret it's Box 1's shape an octave up — don't confuse them.

When & why

  • Bright, cutting register for solos and climaxes.
  • Sits over the E-form barre — easy chord-tone targeting.
  • Bridges Box 3 to Box 5.

Moves & transitions

  • Slide down into Box 3 or up into Box 5.
  • The 12th-fret octave marker keeps you oriented.
  • Big, vocal bends live well up here.
Practice tipUse the fret markers (12) as your anchor. Loop Box 4 to a click until the higher position feels as safe as fret 5.

“Up here the strings are tighter — bends need more push. Tune your ear to the target pitch, not the effort.”
